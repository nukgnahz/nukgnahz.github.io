Overview

Before I joined Beihang University, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow and Research Fellow with Prof. Ji-Feng Zhang (IEEE, Fellow) and Prof. Rong Su (IEEE Senior Member) at Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences and School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU). I received the PhD degree in Control Theory and Control Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Huaguang Zhang (IEEE, Fellow) at Northeastern University. I am now the Doctoral and Master's Supervisor at Beihang University, welcome to contact me by email.

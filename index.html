About me

I received the B.S. and M.Sc. degrees in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics, Management Science and Engineering from Hebei Normal University, and the Northwest University for Nationalities, China, in 2012 and 2015, respectively. In April 2020, I received the PhD degree in Control Theory and Control Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Huaguang Zhang at Northeastern University. I am a Postdoctoral Fellow and Research Fellow with Prof. Ji-Feng Zhang and Prof. Rong Su at Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences and Nanyang Technological University (NTU).
